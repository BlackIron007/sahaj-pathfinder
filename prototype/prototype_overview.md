# Sahaj PathFinder: Executive Platform Overview

**Agentic MSME Acquisition Intelligence Platform**

This document provides a high-level overview of the Sahaj PathFinder platform interfaces, illustrating the journey from ecosystem discovery to executive impact tracking.

## Screen 1: Discovery Dashboard

**Purpose:** The central command center for Relationship Managers (RMs) to monitor ecosystem growth and review AI-flagged acquisition opportunities.

* **Opportunity Tracking:** Surfaces new MSME discoveries and prioritizes high-intent prospects ready for review.
* **Route Distribution:** Visualizes the breakdown of recommended acquisition pathways (Transaction, Advisor, Anchor, Direct) across the ecosystem.
* **Network Mapping:** Provides a topological snapshot of newly identified network nodes and connected entities.
* **Actionable Recommendations:** Highlights specific target MSMEs along with the AI’s recommended route and relevant SBI product offering.

## Screen 2: Acquisition Intelligence

**Purpose:** A deep-dive analytical view of a single target MSME, exposing the agent's reasoning, signal detection, and ecosystem mapping.

* **Context & Signals:** Evaluates unstructured data to identify operational realities, such as working capital stress, digital readiness, and existing anchor/advisor relationships.
* **Decision Rationale:** Provides transparent, step-by-step reasoning explaining exactly why the AI selected a specific acquisition route over the alternatives.
* **Route Evaluation:** Displays the comparative AI confidence levels across all four available acquisition routes.
* **Ecosystem Context:** Estimates the future network yield, showing how acquiring this single node could unlock visibility into downstream sub-suppliers.

## Screen 3: Offer Workspace

**Purpose:** The execution layer where the Relationship Manager reviews the AI-generated strategy, ensures compliance, and launches the personalized outreach.

* **Strategy Alignment:** Maps the selected route to the most appropriate SBI product (e.g., MSME Sahaj financing, SME loans).
* **Automated Compliance:** Instantly runs pre-checks against internal banking policies, risk signals, and product eligibility criteria.
* **AI-Generated Outreach:** Drafts hyper-personalized communication tailored to the prospect, leveraging their specific friction points and anchor relationships instead of generic marketing.
* **Deployment Workflow:** Allows the RM to approve the strategy and launch the automated digital onboarding journey with a single click.

## Screen 4: Impact Center

**Purpose:** Executive-level analytics tracking the macro business impact and the continuous learning loop of the AI models.

* **Macro Performance:** Tracks aggregate outcomes including new MSME acquisitions, loan book expansion, and reduction in customer acquisition costs.
* **Route Efficacy:** Compares the overall success rates and revenue generated across the different acquisition routes over time.
* **Agent Insights & Flywheel:** Highlights strategic learnings (e.g., shifting trends between liquidity-led vs. advisor-led conversions) to continuously adapt and improve future routing decisions.
* **Ripple Effect Tracking:** Showcases specific network success stories, visualizing how a single initial acquisition successfully cascaded into the discovery of multiple new ecosystem nodes.
